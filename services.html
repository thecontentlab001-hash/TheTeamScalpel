Minimally Invasive

Laparoscopic Surgery

Pioneering minimally invasive techniques that utilize a tiny camera and specialized instruments inserted through minor incisions.

Elite Benefits

  • Significantly less post-operative pain
  • Minimal scarring compared to open surgery
  • Shorter hospital stays (often outpatient/day-care)
  • Faster return to normal activities

Procedure Overview

Performed under general anesthesia. Small 0.5cm incisions are made to accommodate the laparoscope, and high-definition monitors are used to perfectly guide Dr. Agarwal's movements.
